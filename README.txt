Installation instructions:


	#1 - Upload the loadingscreen to your Webserver.
	
	#2 - Open the "install.php". E.g. - (your_server.example/loadingscreen/install.php)
		#2.1 - You'll also be redirected to the "install.php", if you open the loadingscreen for the first time!
		
	#3 - Create an admin account.
		#3.1 - If you decided to save your account-informations as cleartext, you'll find it in "assets/temp/admin_cleartext.txt".
	
	#4 - Configure it to your wishes.
		#4.1 - If you don't know where to find the STEAM_API_KEY, take a look here: http://steamcommunity.com/dev/apikey
		
	#5 - Configure the News to your wishes. You'll find them in the "inc" Folder. It's at the bottom of the "config.inc.php".
		#5.1 - I promise to open it with notepad++ or a similar program.
		
	#6 - Check if the loadingscreen fits your benefits. Therefor use the preview or take a look at it: e.g. - (your_server.example/loadingscreen/index.php)
	
	#7 - Add the loadingscreen to your gmod server. It needs to be set in the "autoexec.cfg".
			e.g: sv_loadingurl "http://your_server.example/loadingscreen/?steamid=%s&mapname=%m"
			
	
	Usefull Link to setup the loadingscreen on your gmod server:
			- https://wiki.garrysmod.com/page/Loading_URL
